How MeritQuant Makes Decisions

Macro Regime Gate

Before any trade, the system reads VIX, yield curve (10Y-2Y), DXY, and HY credit spreads. A RISK-OFF regime blocks all entries except hedges. This is why some sessions show zero trades — the AI is protecting capital.

Signal Scoring (0–10)

Each ticker is scored across RSI (14-period), SMA 20/50/200 alignment, volume ratio, news catalyst tier (1=FDA/contract, 4=noise), and Reddit sentiment. Minimum score of 6.5 for Tier 2 consideration.

Claude Opus 4.8 Decision

Top signals plus macro context are passed to Claude Opus 4.8. The AI scores conviction 1-10, writes a full thesis, identifies the catalyst, maps the technical setup, and flags risk factors. Minimum conviction of 6/10 to trade.

Memory System

Every losing trade's lesson is stored and injected into the next decision prompt. The AI actively avoids repeating documented mistakes — thesis errors, poor timing, sector overlap — improving over time.
